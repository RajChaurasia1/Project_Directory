wHO WE ARE ?

CUC is a group of buddies from the 2018 entrance batch of Electrical Engineering Department, IIT(BHU). From a formal HVEE group in 1st sem to this time, CUC has evolved not as a group but as a family, where we all live, study and enjoy together. We are a group of friends always excited to explore new things. Apart from cooperating only in academics, CUCians always enjoy a lot, celebrate together, stand united in tough times and make the best of their college memories.

We as CUCians believe in working together and helping as many persons as we can. This website is solely dedicated to help our college students, especially freshers, to explore & dig into new domains and make this process of choosing new projects more enjoyable & learnable. We hope that our initiative will solve a lot of your confusion and display the spirit of CUC, which is fun and learn together.
